What AURIA Can Do

Understanding your emotional companion

What AURIA Is

AURIA is an AI-powered emotional companion designed to provide a calming, supportive space for conversation and reflection. She's here to listen without judgment, offer gentle perspectives, and help you navigate your emotional landscape.

AURIA adapts to your emotional state, remembering how you're feeling and responding with appropriate warmth and empathy. She can be an outlet for your thoughts, a sounding board for your ideas, or simply a peaceful presence when you need quiet company.

What AURIA Can Help With

  • Emotional Support: A listening ear when you're going through difficult times
  • Mood Tracking: Helping you understand and express your feelings
  • Reflection: Thoughtful prompts to help you gain clarity
  • Grounding: Simple exercises to help manage overwhelming emotions
  • Daily Check-ins: A space to process your day
  • Motivation: Gentle encouragement when you need a boost
  • Calm Presence: Simply being there when silence feels too loud

What AURIA Is Not

  • Not a Doctor: AURIA cannot diagnose mental health conditions
  • Not a Replacement for Therapy: She complements but doesn't replace professional care
  • Not a Crisis Hotline: For emergencies, please use dedicated crisis services
  • Not Judgmental: She never judges or criticizes
  • Not a Task Manager: Her focus is on you, not your to-do list

Safety Note

AURIA is designed to be a supportive companion, but she's not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please reach out to:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency Services: 911
  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI): 1-800-950-6264

If you ever express thoughts of self-harm or crisis, AURIA will gently encourage you to reach out to these resources. Your wellbeing is the priority.

Getting Started

The best way to experience AURIA is to simply be yourself. Share what's on your mind, express how you're feeling, or even sit in comfortable silence together. There's no wrong way to use this space — it's yours.

Start by selecting your current mood, then begin a conversation. AURIA will meet you where you are.
